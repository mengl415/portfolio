Creating a new brand identity from scratch as part of my EastMeetsWest Internship.

I collaborated with my colleague Supriya Narayanan to create the brand guidelines for Seoul-based B2B marketing agency EastMeetsWest. My primary responsibilities included the visual aspects, such as logo design, moodboard creation, color palette, typography pairings, applications, and mockups.

Time: 10.1.2021 to 11.15.2021

Overview: The client filled out a brand questionnaire which included details such as descriptive adjectives, company purpose, and demographics, which was the starting point for the brand identity creation.

TARGET AUDIENCE
The target audience is the global market, with a focus on the B2B segment in the Beauty & Lifestyle sector. The main target audience is within Korea, but we aim to have a global target market with clientele from outside of Korea as well.

Clients would include businesses who might already have marketing techniques in plac or are still in beginning stages but have not been garnering enough positive sales or reach with their current strategies. Clients would also include new businesses looking to establish their brands in the market.
BRAND VISION & MISSION
East Meets West believes that the world we live in is one big puzzle, merely scattered into different pieces. With the universal language of marketing, we aim to put the right pieces of the world's puzzle back to their original piece.

Through our various creative marketing activities, we aim to bridge the gap between the east and the west and become one of the key players in bringing the world closer.
BRAND ELEMENTS
The logo embodies the brand's tones of being modern, clean, and abstract. The geometric letters joined together abstractly represents the brand's idea of connecting the east to the west, with the sea green acting as a subtle nod to nature.
The idea behind the color palette and moodboard is to encapsulate EastMeetsWest's aim of bringing two worlds together. The primary colors of sea green and light blue represent the west and east respectively, as well as natural elements of the world, namely the sky and earth.
