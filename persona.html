Persona 5 Wikibook, a project to turn a 20,000+ word Wikipedia page into a book and website.

Class: Typography Studio, Fall 2020

Time: 10.22.2020 to 12.12.2020

Project: Choose a Wikipedia page of at least 20,000 words to translate into a print and digital format.

My chosen page was on the video game Persona 5, and I incorporated elements and themes from the game itself into the design of the book, such as the black, red, and white color scheme, the table of contents' similarity to a menu screen, and the recurring use of irregular rectanglular frames. The style of offset irregular rectangles behind text and frames is inspired by the game's UI and text style. A major theme of the game centers around the teenage characters' subtle ways of rebelling against society, which I chose to incorporate through an asymmetrical grid and then breaking the grid in certain spreads.
