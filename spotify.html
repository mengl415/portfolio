Spotify Karaoke, an iterative redesign of the Spotify app purposed for karaoke.

Especially in the wake of a global panic, social gatherings for karaoke can be difficult to coordinate. We redesigned Spotify as an on-the-go, at-home karaoke app, beginning with background research on both Spotify and karaoke and finishing with the redesigned user interface.

Class: Digital Product Design, Fall 2021 Time: 10.1.2021 to 11.15.2021

Team: Lily Meng, Fazil Quaisar, Sherry Huang

Project: Redesign a digital product (Spotify) with an individual focus on a specific audience or use case.

Responsibilities: idea and feature conceptualization, competitor analysis, Spotify research, key screens
