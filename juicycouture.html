    Juicy Couture is an LA-based clothing brand known for the iconic Juicy velour tracksuit, growing to become a global fashion company as a casual luxury brand. As part of my internship, I was tasked with designing a new Y2K direction for Juicy Couture Icons, encompassing logo designs, clothing hangtags, joker fit pant tags, and woven labels, along with general social media and email marketing advertisements for the 2022 holiday season.

    moodboard

    I began with a moodboard to take stock of Y2K trends and get an overview of various potential colors, textures, and assets to create and use. This helped inspire new ideas for logo and packaging when I was stuck, as well as provided multiple visual directions to explore and refine.

    logo designs

    Tasked with designing with the current brand guidelines, such as the typeface used for "Juicy Couture," and encouraged to use the heritage symbols (the dog, cherry, and crown) as well as create my own, I began designing in Illustrator and exploring every possibility I could think of. I combined some with gradients that I had created, as well as holographic and colorful stock textures I found, and ended up choosing a smaller group of logos to present.

    clothing tags

    For the clothing tags, I was tasked to design using both the Y2K direction as well as current Juicy heritage symbols, so I combined the two in various ways, such as Y2K colors and textures with heritage symbols or vice versa. I also played with the physical texturing and layering of the packaging for the hangtags, designing potential vinyl or other transparent material patterned with symbols, as well as cutouts of the symbols.
