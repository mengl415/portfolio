Speculative design project to rethink your closet and fashion.

In a modern, digital age where so many aspects of an individual's life are made more efficient through digital means, it can be hard to streamline the process of getting ready in the morning.

With NextFit, we aim to give users an easier way to choose an outfit each morning, as well as to visualize and improve their space's organization.

Class: Digital Product Design, Fall 2021

Team: Lily Meng, Fazil Quaisar, Sherry Huang, Cathy Shi

Project: Design a digital product based on a future state that does not exist; it should have a point of view of how the world could be and the society within it could be in the near future.

Responsibilities: idea conceptualization, ideation, design system, key screens (team), design consistency

Goals for users: to digitize their closets, easily curate outfits, find fashion inspiration from current closets, and understand their organizational and purchasing habits
